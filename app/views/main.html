What's so special about this sitemap generator?

This crawler will execute your javascript. So its perfect for generating sitemaps for sites built with angular, react, or any other popular .js framework.

How to use this sitemap generator

In order for the crawler to properly scan your website. Place this script at the bottom of your site before the closing body tag. You can remove this script after your sitemap is generated.

Where do I submit my sitemap?

Submit your sitemap to Google using the Search Console Sitemaps tool.

Common Issues

  • The script above must be included on all the pages of your website. Placing it in the footer before the closing body tag is best practice.
  • Passing the "X-Frame-Options" header will prevent the crawler from working properly. Please make sure you are not passing this header back in your page responses.
  • The sitemap generator may experience problems on pages that take longer than 10 seconds to load. In order to crawl javascript initiated content the crawler simulates browser behavior.

About

This sitemap generator is built with 100% javascript. Its designed to simulate a browser and crawl content, especially content originating from javascript.

Other sitemap generators have issues crawling sites built with javascript frameorks such as angular and react.
